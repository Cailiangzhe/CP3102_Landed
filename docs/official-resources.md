# Official Resource Audit

## Purpose

This document records verified official resources for the **Landed** web app.  
Each public link below was reviewed on **2026-06-13** and should be checked again before each new intake.  
Landed is a **navigator, not an authority**. It links students to official sources rather than replacing university, government, medical, legal or financial advice.

## Before Arrival

### Student's Pass / IPA Documents

**Stage:** Before Arrival  
**Official Sources:**  
- JCU Singapore — Admissions for International Students  
- JCU Singapore — Student's Pass Application Guide  
- Immigration & Checkpoints Authority (ICA) — Becoming a Student's Pass Holder  
- ICA — Completion of Formalities and Issuance of Student's Pass  

**URLs:**  
- https://www.jcu.edu.sg/courses-and-study/admissions/admissions-procedures-And-requirements/admissions-international-students  
- https://www.jcu.edu.sg/current-students/students-pass-application-guide  
- https://www.ica.gov.sg/reside/STP  
- https://www.ica.gov.sg/reside/STP/collect  

**Why Students Need It:** International students need to understand the official Student's Pass process and prepare the documents requested by ICA and JCU before travelling.  
**Plain-English Summary:** Follow the official JCU and ICA instructions. Keep your IPA letter and required documents ready. Complete the required Student's Pass formalities according to the instructions in your IPA letter and the ICA appointment process.  
**Last Verified:** 2026-06-13  
**Notes / Risk:** Visa-related content is high risk. Do not provide legal advice or rewrite detailed eligibility rules. Link directly to the official pages.
