# Week 4 Resource Audit Summary

## Status

The initial official-resource audit for the **Landed** web app has been completed.  
Public links were reviewed on **2026-06-13** and grouped by onboarding stage.

## Key Findings

- Essential first-month information is distributed across JCU Singapore pages, government portals and service-provider websites.
- Some tasks require a clear sequence because later actions may depend on earlier preparation.
- High-risk topics must link directly to official sources and avoid giving legal, medical, visa or financial advice.
- Every public link should display or retain a `Last Verified` date.
- Commercial topics such as SIM cards and bank accounts require neutral wording and should avoid biased recommendations.

## Verified Priority Topics

### Before Arrival
- Student's Pass / IPA documents
- Accommodation preparation
- Arrival pick-up preparation

### Arrival Day
- Immigration clearance / SG Arrival Card
- Public transport
- Local SIM card

### First Week
- Bank account preparation
- Singpass
- LearnJCU and student email
- Attendance requirement

### First Month
- Healthcare and insurance
- Longer-term accommodation
- Day-to-day budgeting
- Campus support services

## Main Risks

- Official links or requirements may change between intakes.
- Visa, attendance, banking and healthcare wording may be misinterpreted if it is too detailed or too informal.
- Commercial recommendations may appear biased if the team promotes specific providers without a transparent comparison method.
- Student-only JCU links may require login and should be checked before appearing in the public app.
- Contact details may change and should be re-checked before launch.
