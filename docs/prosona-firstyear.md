# Lightweight Persona

## 1. Persona Overview

### Name

Lina Chen

### Age

19

### Role

First-year international student at JCU Singapore

### Current Situation

Lina has recently arrived in Singapore to begin her university studies. This is her first time living independently in another country.

She receives information from university emails, official websites, group chats, and advice from friends. Although information is available, she is unsure which tasks are urgent and which tasks can be completed later.

She mainly uses her mobile phone to search for information.

---

## 2. Background

Lina needs to complete several tasks during her first month in Singapore, including:

* preparing documents;
* understanding arrival procedures;
* attending orientation;
* checking Student's Pass requirements;
* learning how to use university systems; and
* settling into daily life.

She wants a simple platform that helps her understand what to do next without reading long pages from multiple websites.

---

## 3. Goals

Lina wants to:

* understand which tasks should be completed first;
* avoid missing important deadlines;
* track completed tasks;
* find short explanations in simple language;
* access reliable official links;
* use the platform easily on her phone; and
* feel more confident during her first month in Singapore.

---

## 4. Pain Points

Lina experiences several problems:

* Important information is spread across different websites and emails.
* Some official pages contain too much text.
* She is not always sure which source is reliable.
* She does not know which tasks are urgent.
* She may forget whether she has already completed a task.
* She does not want to share personal information with an unfamiliar website.
* She mainly uses her phone, so complicated layouts are inconvenient.

---

## 5. Needs

Lina needs:

* a four-stage checklist;
* visible progress tracking;
* short and clear explanations;
* direct links to official sources;
* a mobile-friendly interface;
* an FAQ page for quick answers; and
* a platform that does not request sensitive personal information.

---

## 6. Behaviour

Lina usually:

* searches for information using her mobile phone;
* asks classmates for advice when official instructions are unclear;
* saves useful links in browser tabs or chat messages;
* prefers short instructions before opening a detailed official page;
* checks urgent tasks first; and
* feels more comfortable when she can see her progress.

---

## 7. Quote

> “I do not need another long guide. I just want to know what I should do first and where to find the correct official information.”

---

## 8. User Scenario

Lina opens the Landed website after arriving in Singapore.

On the home page, she sees four stages: Before Arrival, Arrival Day, First Week, and First Month.

She selects **First Week** and opens the checklist. She sees urgent tasks and marks orientation as completed.

She then opens the Student's Pass checklist item, reads a short explanation, and follows the official link for the latest information.

When she returns to the website later, her completed task is still marked. This helps her understand what remains unfinished.

---

## 9. Design Implications

| Persona Need                                    | Design Response                                                         |
| ----------------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Lina does not know which tasks are urgent.      | Organise tasks into four clear stages.                                  |
| Lina may forget completed tasks.                | Save checklist progress in the browser.                                 |
| Lina finds long instructions difficult to read. | Use short explanations and link to official pages.                      |
| Lina mainly uses her phone.                     | Apply a mobile-first layout with clear buttons and cards.               |
| Lina wants reliable information.                | Use verified official links and show clear source labels.               |
| Lina is concerned about privacy.                | Avoid collecting names, IDs, contact details, or sensitive information. |
